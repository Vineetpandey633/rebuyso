Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our buyback and recycling loops.

How do you calculate asset rates?

We evaluate your scrap items or used electronics based on current global commodity spot markets and system operational conditions to give you top market value.

Is my corporate data secure during IT buybacks?

Yes, absolutely. All electronics with data storage undergo strict data sanitization or physical destruction compliance processing cycles before being recycled or resold.

Is there a pickup charge or minimum weight requirement?

Doorstep collection is completely free for all verified corporate and industrial lots. For residential pickups, minimum quantity thresholds vary based on your specific location parameters.

How fast do I receive payments and compliance documents?

Payments are cleared instantly via electronic fund transfers immediately after final physical valuation confirmation. Green recycling certifications and audit summaries follow within 48-72 business hours.
